Where Speed Meets Accuracy: TextTornado's Typing Velocity!


Your goal is to type the given text as quick and accurately as possible.

Each challenge has a set time limit of 60 seconds.

Your score will be based on your Words Per Minute(WPM) typing speed. Accurancy is also important, so try to minimize mistakes. If you exceeds more than 15 mistakes your typing results will not be processed

There will be sets of events where you will be required to type the text as shown in the typing area, including punctuations and spaces.

After tyiping the texts check the leaderboard to see how you rank against other players.

Correctly typed words will turn yellowgreen, and mistakes will be highlighted to red. So type carefully as each incorrect keystrokes or typo deductes points from your score.

We'll track your progress over time. Use this information to see how you're improving by hecking on history section.

If you encounter technical issues or have feedback, contact our support team at capsualcode@gmail.com or use support section provided.

We take your privacy seriously. Read our privacy policy to understand how your data is handled. Most significantly, have fun and challenge yourself to become a better typist and win rewards at the end of every event.
